Solution

Intentionally Minimal and Mindfully Mobile

For everything, the designer must be afforded the patience, time, and resources to ask, “Why?”.

Poor and excessive design, sensory overload, incoherent, off-brand, irrelevant data, needless complexity, purposeless features, performance deprivation—all contribute to bad design and negative experiences for the end-user. Everything should be intentional and well-executed. Users should be presented with everything necessary to make the desired decisions. Nothing less--or more.
